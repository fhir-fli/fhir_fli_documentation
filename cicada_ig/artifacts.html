Structures: Resource Profiles

These define constraints on FHIR resources for systems conforming to this implementation guide.

Allergy Intolerance Profile for Immunization Decision Support

Profile for allergy intolerances relevant to immunization. The code (allergen/substance) may use SNOMED CT or ICD-10-CM. Reaction substances may use CVX, MVX, SNOMED CT, or RxNorm.
Condition Profile with Vaccine Condition Codes

Profile for conditions where the code must be from the Vaccine Condition Codes value set. Accepts SNOMED CT and ICD-10-CM coded conditions.
Dose of a Vaccine

Detailed information about each vaccination dose.
Immunization Procedures Profile

Profile for medical procedures related to immunization based on SNOMED CT or CPT codes.
Medication Administration Profile with Vaccine Codes

Profile for medication administrations with vaccine codes from CVX or MVX.
Medication Dispense Profile with Vaccine Codes

Profile for medication dispenses with vaccine codes from CVX or MVX.
Medication Request Profile with Vaccine Codes

Profile for medication requests with vaccine codes from CVX or MVX.
Medication Statement Profile with Vaccine Codes

Profile for medication statements with vaccine codes from CVX or MVX.
Observation Profile for Immunization Decision Support

Profile for Observation resources carrying lab evidence of immunity or other findings relevant to immunization decisions. Codes may be from SNOMED CT, ICD-10-CM, or LOINC.
Vaccination Patient

A profile that extends the base FHIR Patient resource to include detailed vaccination-related information.
Vaccine

Simple vaccine to easily obtain needed information for forecasting

Captures the status of the allowed interval for vaccination.

Captures the reason why a particular vaccine is allowed.

Indicates if the vaccine administered is allowed under certain conditions.
AssessmentDate

The age at which the vaccine becomes applicable.
End Age

The age at which the vaccine is no longer applicable.
Inadvertent Administration Status

Indicates if the vaccine was administered inadvertently.
Preferred Interval Reason

Captures the reason for the preferred interval between vaccine doses.
Preferred Interval Status

Preferred Vaccine Reason

Captures the reason why a particular vaccine is preferred.
Preferred Vaccine Status

Indicates if the vaccine administered is the preferred vaccine.
Vaccination Conflict

Indicates any conflicts with other vaccinations.
Vaccine Type

Type of the vaccine.
Valid Age Reason

Valid Age Status

Indicates if the vaccine was administered at the correct age.
