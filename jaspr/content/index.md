## FHIR-FLI — FHIR with Flutter

FHIR-FLI is a suite of Dart libraries for working with [FHIR](https://www.hl7.org/fhir/), the healthcare interoperability standard. All packages support **FHIR R4, R5, and R6** with identical APIs — just swap the package name.

- **[About](about)** — what FHIR-FLI is and who it's for
