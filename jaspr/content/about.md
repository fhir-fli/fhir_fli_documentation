## About FHIR-FLI

FHIR-FLI (Fast Healthcare Interoperability Resources with Flutter Library Integration) is an open-source collaborative with the goal of improving healthcare through the use of the FHIR® interoperability standard and the Flutter Toolkit. We believe that lowering the barrier (both technically and monetarily) to entry allows for more innovation, broader applications, and the ability to actually use technology to improve people's lives.

Please feel free to join our Slack channel, or email me directly at [grey@fhirfli.dev](mailto:grey@fhirfli.dev).
